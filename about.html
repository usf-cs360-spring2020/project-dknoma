Why Steam?

Steam is the largest video game distribution service for PC gaming. There are so many different AAA games available on various consoles or platforms, and often times they are available on Steam. Being that I use Steam very often I figured why not visualize some data from the platform I use the most. This dataset has many of the important fields that make up a review on Steam.
