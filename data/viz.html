Implementation

Description

Each data point represents each Parking Enforcement incident. The color is used to make it easier to tell the data points from the background map. All the lines on the map represent a limited number of streets in each district in San Francisco.

Interactivity

Hover over a district will highlight that district in red, showing the name of the district near the user's cursor. When hovering over a data point a table of details will be displayed at the top left corner of the visualization. This table shows the details of an incident:

  • Incident id
  • The date the incident was opened
  • The district name
  • Service Name (Parking Enforcement)
  • The details of the car (if applicable)

Conclusion

There seems to be a higher correlation of Parking Enforcement incidents in areas that have a higher street density as well as being closer to major streets/highways (the wide white streets). This is likely due to the high amount of traffic the area gets which means that there is a higher chance that someone is there to enforce parking violations.
